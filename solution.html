What are the solutions to protect and make our earth clean?


Save and plant trees

The first step that we all should take to protect our planet that is to plant more trees. Start by planting some trees on your house. It will help the earth for years to produce more oxygen


Seperating garbages

Try to seperate the Biodegradable and the Non-Biodegradable garbages into seperate dustbin. As Non-biodegradable garbages do not decompose


Inexhaustible resources

Try to use inexhaustible resources for fuels and electricity. There are two advantage of it: One is it stops increase of co2 and the dependency on fossil fuel get reduced.


Spread Awareness

Spread this Awareness news among your fellow residents. This will make the process more faster. If you see anyone is cutting trees stop him from doing that. Tell your neighbours to throw garbage in proper dustbin.
